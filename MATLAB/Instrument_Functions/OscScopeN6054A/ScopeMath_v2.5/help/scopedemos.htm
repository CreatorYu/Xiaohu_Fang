Oscilloscope Demos

These demos show you how to communicate with your oscilloscope using MATLAB and the Instrument Control Toolbox. There are two types of demos:

  • Simple oscilloscope communication
  • Application examples

To view or run MATLAB demos, open the MATLAB demos entry. To view or run Instrument Control Toolbox demos, open the Toolboxes demos entry and then select Instrument Control.

Note that to communicate with your instrument, you must have a compatible VISA driver installed on your computer.
